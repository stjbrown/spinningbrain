# Test Brain Charter

## Purpose

This is a general-purpose test Brain used to validate the Brain Agent.

## Audience

Write for the internal team evaluating and developing Spinning Brain.

## Knowledge Model

- Organize knowledge by durable topics rather than by conversation or date.
- Preserve implementation decisions, discoveries, and unresolved questions.
- Connect related concepts when doing so helps future work.

## Priorities

- Favor clarity and usefulness over completeness.
- Capture decisions and their reasoning.
- Surface unresolved questions instead of silently guessing.

## Specialized Workflows

No additional Brain-specific workflows are configured yet.
